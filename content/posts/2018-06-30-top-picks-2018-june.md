[Ken Wheeler](https://twitter.com/ken_wheeler) about the open source contribution — the bitter reality and struggle to maintain a free projects for the community. A great list of tips for all OSS creators.

## [A bright future for GitHub](https://blog.github.com/2018-06-04-github-microsoft/)

[Chris Wanstrath](https://twitter.com/defunkt) from GitHub team announced the biggest acquisition in a long time. This one is importnt especificaly for developers as of today (2018.06.04) GitHub joins a parent company — Microsoft. Microsoft being an creator and maintainer of core open source projects (TypeScript & VSCode) this one was highly possible but not at all expected. I am looking forward what a future of this acquisition will bring to the table. Satya Nadella, Chief Executive Officer at Microsoft reveals more details in ["Microsoft + GitHub = Empowering Developers"](https://blogs.microsoft.com/blog/2018/06/04/microsoft-github-empowering-developers/) on a Microsoft' blog.
